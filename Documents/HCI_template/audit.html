WebMD

WebMD is a website and a mobile application that provides a bunch of free and so said reliable information on medications in four categories: the symptom checker, find a doctor, drugs and supplements, and expert blogs and interviews credible as it describes. Furthermore, WebMD does not offer any local information about possible diseases in the community.

Everyday Health with Acupressure - Daily Massage!

Everyday Health with Acupressure - Daily Massage! is an mobile application aiming to use massage to help people relieve cough, cold, sore throat, heartburn, constipation, diarrhoea, toothache, sleeplessness, fever, and depression. It provides clear directions and videos for users to learn and do the massages. However, this method can only work for some users. When their symptoms becomes severe, users have to drop this app and go to hospitals in the end.
