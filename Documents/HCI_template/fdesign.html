Learning Technology Design Project

Functional Design

Functional Elements

We are providing a website which is accessible from different types of devices such as smartphones, tablets, laptops, and computers. By using the responsive design concepts, we are certifying that people will have the pleasant experience using our website even on the small screens.

Functional Requirement Actual Design Element
Create a User Profile In the beginning, users are asked to enter their credentials to login or sign up. They are also able to continue as the guest but if they want to archive the information they should sign up first. The login property will be accessible in all screens to make sure users are not confused where they should go.
Customize Alerts about the outbreak of communicable diseases Users will be asked to agree that app can browse the location so they can use this data for customizing the alerts based on the location dynamically.
Notify about top 3 marked symptoms of the disease Once users are making comments on the disease page, they will be asked to make the symptoms they had. This information will be shown to the users on the disease page. It is showing anonymously.
Engage with community The community has been designed in the main menu so users are able to navigate to it easily.
